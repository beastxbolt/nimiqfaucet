About Us

Nimiq Faucet is a faucet website that collects nimiq tokens from contributors, then redistributes those tokens in small amounts to users who want a share of it for themselves. Our primary goal is to help people who have no, or few, getting some free nimiq tokens to start their journey. A user can request for nimiq tokens every 15 min. Tokens are paid instantly in your Nimiq wallet and the transaction fees are paid by us.

We collect Nimiq tokens through donations and revenues from ads running on the site which helps us pay the deployment and maintenance cost of the website.
