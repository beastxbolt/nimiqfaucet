Where do the Nimiq tokens come from?

All the Nimiq tokens in the faucet come from donations from contributors and ad revenues. Even I donate myself to the faucet from the ad revenues.

What is a faucet?

A faucet in cryptocurrency term is a website that gives crypto coins/tokens, a small amount of coins/tokens at a time (called drops). The main purpose is to help people who are new to the community get their first coins/tokens. Most faucets usually restrict the number of times you can request for coins/tokens with time limitation. With Nimiq Faucet you can request for tokens every 15 min.

I just claimed tokens, but haven't recieved them in the wallet.

Nimiq tokens are sent instantly but it takes atleast 1 confirmation to authorise the transaction and send it in your wallet. Until then, a pending transaction should be visible in your wallet transactions. If it doesn't, make sure you entered the correct wallet address while claiming the tokens.

It says wrong wallet address when I try to claim the tokens.

Nimiq wallet address has space after every 4 digits of the address. Make sure you do NOT remove those spaces or your address will be marked as invalid.

How do I get a Nimiq address?

To receive Nimiq, you need a wallet. You can use Nimiq's official web wallet on wallet.nimiq.com or you can use any other wallet and get your Nimiq address from there.

Is Nimiq Faucet really free?

Yes, it is 100% free to use. The website's maintenance and hosting costs are paid by the ad revenues.
